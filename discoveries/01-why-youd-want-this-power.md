# Why You'd Want This Power — Discovery Notes

- **Problem:** Sparks, shocks, twitching frog legs, glowing wire — all seem related. What single invisible thing explains them, and how do you measure it well enough to use it?
- **Key idea:** Electricity has two independent handles: how hard it pushes (voltage) and how much flows per moment (current). Power is both together — P = V × I. You can't describe or design without both.
