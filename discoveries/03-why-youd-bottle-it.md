# Why You'd Bottle It — Discovery Notes

- **Problem:** Electricity only flows while a source is connected. How do you store the push so you can carry it somewhere else and release it on demand?
- **Key idea:** You can't hold the flow — you hold the pressure that would produce flow. Two ways: charge separated on two plates (capacitor: fast, little total energy) or chemistry maintaining a potential difference (battery: slow, large total energy). Each is right for different jobs.
