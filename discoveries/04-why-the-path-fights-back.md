# Why the Path Fights Back — Discovery Notes

- **Problem:** Every path resists the flow and converts push to heat. Where is the missing energy going, and how do you design around it — or with it?
- **Key idea:** Resistance (V = IR, Ohm's law) is constant for a given material, length, and cross-section. Heat scales as I²R — doubling current quadruples heating. Flip it: engineer a high-resistance section to produce heat or light exactly where you want it (resistor, heating element, incandescent bulb).
