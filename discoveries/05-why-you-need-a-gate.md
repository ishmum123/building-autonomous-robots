# Why You Need a Gate — Discovery Notes

- **Problem:** Electricity flows the instant a path exists — you need "now/not now" and "this way, not backward." How do you control when and which direction?
- **Key idea:** A switch physically interrupts or restores the path (temporal control); a diode uses asymmetric atomic structure at a semiconductor junction to pass current one way and block the other (directional control). Mechanical switches wear from contact arcing — this is the seed of why brushed motors have a limited lifespan.
