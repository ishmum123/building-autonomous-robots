# Why It Should Switch Itself — Discovery Notes

- **Problem:** Every gate needs a human hand — too slow, too few, can't be everywhere. What if a tiny trickle of power could operate a gate on a large flow, without any hand involved?
- **Key idea:** A transistor uses a small control signal to modulate a large output current, with no moving parts and nanosecond switching. The same principle gives amplification, logic, and ultimately the computation that runs every part of your robot — from motor commutation to flight controller to path planning.
