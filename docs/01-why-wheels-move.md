# Why Wheels Move

## The Problem

You have invented the wheel.

It still doesn't move.

Why?

## Challenge

You have:
- One wheel
- A flat floor

How can motion begin?

## Exercise

Sketch three different ways to make the wheel move before reading further.

## Engineering Notes

The wheel alone is not enough. Motion always requires an interaction:

- Force
- Friction
- Constraint

These ideas will appear throughout robotics.
